# India Glycols Limited (IGL) - Potable Spirits Division
## Investor-Grade Landing Page: UX Wireframe Document

**Positioning:** "Where Smooth Meets Sophistication"
**Tonal Ratio:** 60% Investor Relations | 25% Premium Spirits Brand | 15% Manufacturing Excellence
**Primary Audience:** Investors, board members, institutional stakeholders, senior business leaders

---

## 1. HERO SECTION

**Purpose:** Establish gravitas and brand promise within three seconds. This is the handshake, it must feel like an annual report cover, not a liquor ad.

**Content Hierarchy:**
1. Division wordmark / IGL endorsement lockup
2. Tagline: "Where Smooth Meets Sophistication"
3. One-line positioning statement (e.g. "A premium spirits portfolio built on India's leading specialty chemicals and bio-based manufacturing heritage")
4. Primary CTA: "Investor Relations" | Secondary CTA: "Explore the Portfolio"

**Visual Treatment:** Full-bleed editorial photography. Deep charcoal base, warm amber backlight, faint bottle silhouette in negative space. Ivory typography, serif headline + sans-serif subtext.

**Layout:** 100vh, centered or left-anchored text block, generous margins.

**Motion:** Staggered fade-up on text (0.3s intervals), no parallax. CTA has a slow, almost imperceptible amber glow pulse, nothing else moves.

---

## 2. INVESTMENT SNAPSHOT / KEY METRICS

**Purpose:** Immediate credibility signal. Investors want numbers before narrative.

**Content:** 4-6 metric tiles, e.g. revenue contribution, production capacity, YoY growth, distribution footprint (states/countries), quality certifications, market share trajectory.

**Hierarchy:** Section label ("By the Numbers") > metric grid > footnote citing annual report/fiscal year.

**Layout:** 4-column grid (desktop), 2-column (tablet), single column stack (mobile). Ivory metric cards on charcoal field, thin champagne-gold rule lines as dividers.

**Height:** 60-70vh

**Motion:** Counter count-up animation triggered once on scroll-into-view, 0.8s ease-out. No looping.

---

## 3. STRATEGIC ADVANTAGES

**Purpose:** Establish competitive moat: integrated manufacturing, distribution scale, R&D, parent company strength.

**Content:** 4-5 advantage cards (icon + headline + 1-2 line description). Topics: backward integration with IGL's chemicals business, pan-India distribution, in-house R&D and blending expertise, quality compliance, scale economics.

**Layout:** Asymmetric editorial grid, ample whitespace, not a rigid card wall.

**Height:** 70-80vh

**Motion:** Sequential fade-in as user scrolls, subtle hover lift (2-3px, no shadow drama).

---

## 4. PORTFOLIO SHOWCASE (Sticky Scroll Storytelling)

**Purpose:** The brand's emotional core, 25% allocation. Shows craftsmanship without tipping into consumer marketing.

**Layout Pattern:** Two-column sticky scroll.
- Left column (40% width): bottle image, position: sticky, pinned through entire section.
- Right column (60% width): scrolling content panels, one per product.

**Products in order:** Single Reserve Whisky > Amazing Vodka > Soulmate Whiskey

**Per-Product Content Block:**
1. Product name + category tag
2. Flavor profile (3-4 tasting descriptors, short editorial line)
3. Brand positioning statement (single elegant sentence)
4. Market opportunity (target segment + growth stat)

**Height:** Approximately 300vh total (roughly 100vh of scroll distance per product transition, allowing the sticky effect room to breathe).

**Motion:**
- Bottle rotates within a 20-30 degree arc only, interpolated to scroll progress. Never a full spin.
- Background gradient subtly shifts warmth per product (amber intensity varies slightly).
- Content panels crossfade/slide up on transition, cubic ease-in-out, no bounce or elastic easing.

---

## 5. MANUFACTURING & QUALITY PROCESS

**Purpose:** The 15% manufacturing pillar. Builds operational credibility tied to IGL's industrial heritage.

**Content:** Process stepper: grain/raw material sourcing > distillation > aging > blending > bottling > quality control. Include certifications (ISO, FSSAI, export compliance, etc).

**Layout:** Vertical stepper with facility photography, or horizontal scroll-triggered timeline.

**Height:** 90-100vh

**Motion:** Steps reveal sequentially tied to scroll position. A slow line-draw (1.2s ease) connects each stage, evoking precision rather than spectacle.

## BALANCING INVESTOR COMMUNICATION WITH BRAND STORYTELLING

- **Scroll depth allocation:** Investor-weighted sections (snapshot, strategy, market, governance, roadmap, CTA) anchor navigation and dominate total scroll length (~60%). The portfolio showcase occupies a concentrated, immersive ~25%. Manufacturing visuals are woven through multiple sections rather than isolated, delivering the 15% as texture rather than a standalone module.
- **Typography system:** Serif typeface for brand-voice headlines (hero, portfolio, taglines), clean sans-serif for data, labels, and investor copy. This separates "brand world" from "investor world" without breaking visual cohesion.
- **Color discipline:** Charcoal remains the dominant base across all sections, including investor-heavy ones, so the page never feels like a sterile corporate-blue site. Amber and champagne gold are used strictly as accents, never as backgrounds, to avoid generic luxury-cliché territory.
- **Motion discipline:** All animations stay under 1.2 seconds, ease-in-out, and are scroll-triggered rather than autoplay-looped. This reinforces "slow sophistication" and keeps the page feeling authoritative rather than promotional.
